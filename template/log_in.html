ANOMYO

ANOMYO is a cloud accounting software
that simplifies recording transactions, managing payables and receivables,
collecting taxes, closing the books and enabling timely, accurate reporting
and greater control of financial assets.

With real-time access to financial data, you can quickly drill into details to resolve issues
and generate statements and disclosures to comply with multiple regulatory financial compliance requirements.
